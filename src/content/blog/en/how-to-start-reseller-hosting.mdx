## Why Start a Hosting Business?
The demand for high-quality hosting is constant. Every new business needs a website. By becoming a Reseller, you can offer a bundled service (e.g., Web Design + Hosting + SEO) that creates **Recurring Revenue**—the holy grail of business.

### How it Works
1. **Choose a Reseller Plan**: Select a plan based on how many clients you want to host.
2. **Create Hosting Packages**: Decide how much RAM/Storage each of your customers gets.
3. **Set Up WHMCS**: Use our [WHMCS guide](/blog/what-is-whmcs) to automate your billing and account creation.
4. **Branding**: Use **White-Label Nameservers** so your clients see your brand (ns1.yourbrand.com), not ours.

## 3 Strategies for Success

### 1. The Value Bundle
Don't just sell "hosting." Sell "WordPress Optimization Packages." Include pre-installed security, premium templates, and monthly maintenance. You can charge 10x more than a standard hosting plan.

### 2. Focus on a Local Niche
Be the "Best Hosting for Doctors in New York" or "Fastest Hosting for Lawyers in London." Local SEO is much easier to dominate than global hosting terms.

### 3. Exceptional Support
Big hosting companies have slow ticket systems. You can win by offering personalized, high-speed support to your clients via WhatsApp or direct phone calls.
