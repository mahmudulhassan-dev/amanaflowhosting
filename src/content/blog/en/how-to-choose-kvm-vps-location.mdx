## Why Location Matters
Even with light-speed fiber optics, data takes time to travel. A server in New York will always feel slower to a user in Dhaka than a server in Singapore or Bangladesh.

### 1. Target Audience Analysis
Are you selling to a global market or a local one?
- **Local (BD):** Choose our Dhaka or Singapore nodes for &lt;40ms response times.
- **Global:** Use a US or UK node for balanced access across Europe and the Americas.

### 2. Legal and Compliance
Certain industries (like Fintech) require data to be stored within national borders. Always check local regulations before choosing a region.

### 3. Cost-to-Performance Ratio
US regions are often cheaper due to massive competition, but the latency cost might hurt your SEO if your users are overseas.

## Location Strategy
- **E-commerce:** Use a CDN but keep the origin server close to your main office.
- **Apps:** Multi-region deployment is best for high uptime.
- **Games:** Latency is everything. Only choose regions with &lt;30ms jitter.
