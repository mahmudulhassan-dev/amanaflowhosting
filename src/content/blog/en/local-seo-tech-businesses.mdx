## Why Local SEO Matters for Tech
While the internet is global, trust is often local. A business owner in Dhaka or New York is more likely to hire a tech agency or hosting provider that has a local presence. 

### The 3 Core Pillars of Local SEO

#### 1. Google Business Profile (GBP)
This is the most important factor. Ensure your profile is:
- **Verified**: Get that blue checkmark.
- **Optimized**: High-quality interior/exterior office photos and a clear team photo.
- **Active**: Post updates, offers, and responded to every single review (good or bad).

#### 2. Local Citations (NAP)
Your **Name, Address, and Phone Number (NAP)** must be consistent across the web. If your site says "Suite 201" but Yelp says "Building 2," Google will trust you less.

#### 3. Geo-Targeted Content
Create pages specifically for your location. 
*Example: "High-Speed Managed Hosting for Businesses in Dhaka."* Use local landmarks, local events, and local success stories in your copy.

## FAQ
**Q: I don't have a physical office, can I still do Local SEO?**  
A: Yes! You can set up a "Service Area Business" profile in Google Business without showing your home address.

**Q: Does server location matter for Local SEO?**  
A: Yes. If you are targeting users in Bangladesh, having a server in or near South Asia (like our Singapore or India zones) improves local loading speed and relevance.
