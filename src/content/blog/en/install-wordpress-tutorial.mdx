<TLDR>
**Quick Summary:** Installing WordPress manually is old news. Use the Softaculous Apps Installer in your control panel to set up your site, database, and admin account in just a few clicks.
</TLDR>

## Step 1: Log in to your Control Panel
Access your AmanaFlow hosting account and navigate to your control panel (cPanel). Search for the **Softaculous Apps Installer** icon.

## Step 2: Select WordPress
Inside Softaculous, you will see WordPress at the top of the "Top Scripts" list. Click **Install**.

---

## Step 3: Software Setup
- **Choose Protocol**: Always select **https://**.
- **Choose Domain**: Select the domain you want to install WP on.
- **In Directory**: Leave this blank if you want the site on your main domain (e.g., amanaflow.com).

## Step 4: Site Settings
Provide your **Site Name** and **Site Description**. You can change these later in the WordPress settings.

---

## Step 5: Admin Account (Important!)
- **Admin Username**: Do NOT use "admin". Use a unique name for security.
- **Admin Password**: Use a strong combination of letters, numbers, and symbols.
- **Admin Email**: Use an email you check regularly for system notifications.

## Step 6: Choose Language & Plugins
Select your preferred language. We recommend checking the **Limit Login Attempts** plugin for added security.

---

## Step 7: Finalize Installation
Click the **Install** button. Within 60-90 seconds, you will receive two links:
1. Your website URL.
2. Your administrative URL (typically `yourdomain.com/wp-admin`).

## FAQ
**Q: What if I forget my password?**  
A: You can reset it via the WordPress login page or directly through Softaculous.

**Q: Can I install multiple WordPress sites?**  
A: Yes, as long as your hosting plan (e.g., Business or Cloud) supports multiple websites.
