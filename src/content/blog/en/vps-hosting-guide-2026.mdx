## What actually makes a VPS fast?
It's not just the amount of RAM you buy. It's the underlying infrastructure architecture.

### Key Metrics to Evaluate
1. **CPU Steal Percentage:** Are your neighbors stealing your compute cycles?
2. **Drive I/O Limits:** NVMe SSDs in RAID 10 are the only acceptable standard in 2026.
3. **Network Uplink:** Ensure your host provides a 1Gbps dedicated port minimally.
