## What is a DDoS Attack?
Imagine a thousand people trying to walk through a single door at the same time. The door gets jammed, and nobody can get in. That's a **DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)** attack. Hackers use "Botnets" (networks of compromised computers) to overwhelm your server's bandwidth or CPU.

### The Evolution of Attacks in 2026
In 2026, attacks are no longer just "brute force." They are intelligent. **Layer 7 (Application Layer)** attacks mimic real human behavior, making them harder to detect without advanced AI systems.
