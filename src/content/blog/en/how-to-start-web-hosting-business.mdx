## The Business of Recurring Revenue
For web design and dev agencies, building a website is a one-time payment. But what happens after? The client goes to a massive corporation for their hosting. By offering White-Label Hosting, you capture that recurring revenue seamlessly.

### 1. The White-Label Advantage
"White-label" means your clients never see the name AmanaFlow. They see *your* logo on the control panel, *your* custom nameservers (e.g., `ns1.youragency.com`), and they pay *you* directly. 

### 2. Choosing the Right Platform Layer
You have two main paths:
- **Reseller Hosting:** Best for managing 10-100 clients. You get a massive cPanel/WHM environment and slice it up.
- **Dedicated Server + VM:** Best for 100+ clients or resource-heavy apps. You virtualize your own instances using customized hardware.

### 3. Billing Automation is Key
Do not invoice manually. AmanaFlow Reseller plans integrate perfectly with **WHMCS**, the industry-standard billing panel. It automatically suspends non-paying clients and provisions accounts for new ones in real-time.
