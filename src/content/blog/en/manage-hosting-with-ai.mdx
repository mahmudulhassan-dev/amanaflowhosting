## The Problem with Manual Server Management
Servers are complex. They generate millions of log lines, have thousands of security variables, and can fail for a hundred different reasons. Traditionally, a human "System Administrator" had to watch these manually. 

### Enter Kodee: The AI Admin
We built Kodee to be the world's most proactive server manager. Kodee lives inside our infrastructure, watching your specific VPS or Shared environment.

---

## 3 Core Functions of Kodee

### 1. Predictive Healing
Kodee analyzes your memory and CPU patterns. If it detects a growing bottleneck in a database query, it can suggest optimizations or temporarily reallocate resources to prevent a site crash.

### 2. Autonomous Security
When a new zero-day vulnerability is announced, most sites are vulnerable for hours or days. Kodee receives the security update and automatically hardens your firewall strings against that specific threat in milliseconds.

### 3. Dynamic Optimization
If your target audience is in Europe but they are visiting from Asia today, Kodee can automatically suggest enabling specific edge-caching zones on our global network to ensure they get the best speed.
