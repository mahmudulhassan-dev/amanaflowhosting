## Understanding the Value Chain
Why would someone buy hosting from your agency instead of directly from a mega-corp? **Trust and Support.** Clients are willing to pay a premium when their web developer is also their hosting provider, because they only have one person to call when things break.

### 1. The Cost Structure breakdown
If you lease a high-tier Reseller package from AmanaFlow:
- **Fixed Cost:** $30 to $50 per month.
- **Resource Pool:** 100GB NVMe storage, unmetered bandwidth, and WHM access.

### 2. The Revenue Model
Let's assume you segment that 100GB into 20 accounts of 5GB each.
- **Retail Price:** You charge your clients $15/month for high-performance managed NVMe hosting.
- **Total Revenue:** 20 clients * $15 = $300/month.
- **Profit Margin:** $300 (Revenue) - $50 (Cost) = **$250/month in pure passive profit.**

### 3. Upselling Opportunities
Hosting is just the foundational layer. A smart agency will upsell:
- Weekly offsite backups.
- Premium SSL certificates.
- Dedicated IP addresses.
- Proactive security and plugin updates.

## How to Prevent Profit Margins from Shrinking
The number one killer of reseller profit margins is **support time**. If you are spending 5 hours a week fixing hacked WordPress sites, your effective hourly rate drops.
- **Solution:** Enforce strict security rules (ModSecurity, strong passwords) and utilize AmanaFlow's automated daily JetBackup system so clients can restore files themselves.
