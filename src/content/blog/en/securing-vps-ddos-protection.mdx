## The Threat Landscape
In 2026, botnets can generate Terabits of traffic. Without professional protection, your VPS will be offline within minutes of an attack.

### 1. Hardware Scrubbing (AmanaShield)
At the data center level, we use dedicated hardware scrubbers that analyze every packet. Malicious traffic is filtered out *before* it reaches your server.

### 2. Rate Limiting and WAF
A Web Application Firewall (WAF) helps distinguish between a legitimate customer and a bot attempting to overwhelm your login page.

---

## Hardening Your VPS
We recommend the following steps for all our managed customers:
- Change default SSH ports (avoid port 22).
- Disable root login—use SSH keys instead.
- Enable `Fail2Ban` to block IPs after multiple failed attempts.
