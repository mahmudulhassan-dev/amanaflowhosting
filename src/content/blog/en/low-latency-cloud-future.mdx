## Speed is Distance/Time
In the digital world, distance is measured in network hops. Each hop adds latency. At AmanaFlow, we optimize the physical route your data takes to reach global users.

### 1. Direct Peering (Tier-1)
We peer directly with major ISPs and global backbone providers. This means your data doesn't "zigzag" across the internet—it takes the fastest expressway.

### 2. Edge Computing
By processing data closer to the user at the edge, we reduce the round-trip time (RTT). This is critical for modern AI applications that need instant responses.

**Q: Does my physical location matter?**  
A: Yes. You should choose a data center closest to your *target audience*, not necessarily closer to you.

**Q: Is Cloud faster than VPS?**
