## The Importance of Your Domain
Your domain name is the first thing users see. It's not just an address; it's a branding tool that affects your SEO and user trust. In a world of millions of websites, standing out requires a strategic approach to naming.

### 5 Golden Rules for Picking a Domain

1. **Keep it Short and Simple**: Aim for 6-14 characters. Long names are harder to remember and more prone to typos.
2. **Stick to .com (if possible)**: It’s still the most trusted and recognized extension. If .com is taken, look at **.io**, **.co**, or **.tech** for modern brands.
3. **Make it Pronounceable**: If you have to spell it out every time you say it, it's too complicated.
4. **Avoid Hyphens and Numbers**: They are often misunderstood when spoken and look less professional.
5. **Research Trademarks**: Ensure your "perfect" name doesn't belong to someone else to avoid legal headaches.

---

## The Rise of New TLDs (Top-Level Domains)
In 2026, extensions like **.cloud**, **.ai**, and **.app** are becoming mainstream. They often allow for shorter, more relevant names that are already taken in the .com space. 

### Does it affect SEO?
Google treats most new TLDs the same as .com for ranking purposes. The real SEO value comes from the branding and how users perceive your site.

## Domain vs. Hosting: The Difference
- **Domain**: Your street address (e.g., amanaflow.com).
- **Hosting**: The actual house where your files live.
You need both to have a live website.

## FAQ
**Q: Can I change my domain later?**  
A: Yes, but it involves a complex redirection process (301 redirects) to keep your SEO juice. It's better to pick right the first time.

**Q: How long should I register for?**  
A: We recommend at least 2 years. Search engines often see long-term registrations as a sign of business stability.
