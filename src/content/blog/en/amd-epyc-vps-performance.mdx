In the world of high-traffic sites, the CPU is the brain. If the brain is slow, everything else lags—from database responses to script execution. This is why we've standardizied on **AMD EPYC™**.

### Why AMD EPYC is different

1. **Massive Throughput**: EPYC supports up to 128 cores per socket, meaning your VPS has access to "real" power, not just shared slivers of an old chip.
2. **DDR5 Memory Support**: Faster RAM means your Node.js or WordPress PHP scripts run without waiting for data.
3. **Infinity Architecture**: Reduces data bottlenecks within the CPU, making it perfect for AI and database-heavy applications.
