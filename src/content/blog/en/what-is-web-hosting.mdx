## What exactly is Web Hosting?
Think of your website as a house. To build a house, you need land. In the digital world, **Web Hosting** is that land. It’s a service that allows organizations and individuals to post a website or web page onto the Internet.

A web host, or web hosting service provider, is a business that provides the technologies and services needed for the website or webpage to be viewed in the Internet. Websites are hosted, or stored, on special computers called **servers**.

## Types of Hosting Explained

### 1. Shared Hosting
Perfect for beginners and small blogs. You share server resources with other users. It’s cost-effective but has limits on high-traffic handling.

### 2. VPS (Virtual Private Server) Hosting
The stepping stone to power. You get dedicated resources on a shared physical server. Ideal for growing businesses and developers.

### 3. Cloud Hosting
The ultimate in scalability. Your site is hosted on a cluster of servers, ensuring that if one goes down, another takes over instantly.
