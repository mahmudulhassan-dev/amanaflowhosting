## The Battle of E-commerce Giants
In 2026, the choice between WooCommerce and Shopify is no longer about "which is easier." It's about data ownership and cost efficiency.

### 1. Ownership and Freedom
- **WooCommerce:** Built on WordPress. You own the code. You own the database. No one can ever shut your store down.
- **Shopify:** A hosted platform. You rent your store. If they change their terms, you have to follow or leave.

### 2. Transaction Fees
One of the biggest hidden costs of Shopify is the transaction fee (up to 2% if you don't use Shopify Payments). With WooCommerce on AmanaFlow, you pay **0% transaction fees** to the platform provider.

### 3. SEO and Customization
WooCommerce allows for deep technical SEO optimizations (like custom schemas and server-side edge caching) that are locked away in Shopify.
